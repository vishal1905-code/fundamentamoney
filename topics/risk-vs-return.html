Risk vs Return

What Does Risk Mean?

Risk means the possibility of losing money or not getting the expected return. In investing, risk exists because the future is uncertain.

What Does Return Mean?

Return is the profit you earn from an investment. It can come as interest, dividends, or increase in value.

The Core Rule of Investing

Higher return always comes with higher risk. There is no investment that gives high return with zero risk.

Simple Examples

Fixed Deposit (FD):
Low risk → Low return

Mutual Funds:
Medium risk → Medium return

Stock Market:
High risk → High return potential

Why Understanding Risk Is Important

Many people lose money because they only look at returns and ignore risk. They invest blindly in schemes promising guaranteed high profits.

Important Things Beginners Must Know

  • Never invest without understanding the risk
  • Do not panic during market ups and downs
  • Long-term investing reduces risk
  • Do not chase quick profits

Smart Way to Handle Risk

The best way to manage risk is diversification. This means spreading money across different investments instead of putting everything in one place.
