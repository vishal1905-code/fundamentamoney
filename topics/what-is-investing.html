What is Investing?

Meaning

Investing means using your money in a smart way so that it grows over time. Instead of letting money sit idle, investing allows it to work for you.

How Investing Works

When you invest, you put your money into things like businesses, companies, or financial products. These use your money to create value.

If they perform well, your money increases in value over time.

Why Investing is Important

Simply saving money is not enough because prices keep rising due to inflation. Investing helps your money grow faster than inflation.

Important Things to Know

  • All investments carry some risk
  • Higher returns usually mean higher risk
  • Long-term investing is generally safer
  • Never invest emergency money
